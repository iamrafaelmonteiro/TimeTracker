    Introduction

    Welcome to Time Tracker! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our tool. Time Tracker is a non-profit project developed to help you monitor the time spent on different tasks.

    Information Collection

    Our site collects only the following information:

    • Task Description: The description you provide for each task.
    • Time Intervals: The time you log while working on a specific task.

    This information is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and is not transmitted or stored on external servers.

    Use of Information

    The collected information is used exclusively for:

    • Time Monitoring: Helping you keep track of the time spent on different tasks.
    • Displaying History: Showing a history of the logged time intervals.

    Information Sharing

    We do not share your information with third parties. All data remains in your browser and is accessible only by you.

    Data Storage

    The data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. You can delete all stored data at any time by clicking the "Clear Data" button on our site.

    Security

    Our site does not collect sensitive data. However, we take precautions to protect the information you provide. Since the data is stored locally in your browser, security also depends on your device's settings and security practices.

    Cookies

    We do not use cookies to collect information about users.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this policy occasionally to stay informed about how we protect your information.
